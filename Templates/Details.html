Plant Diseases & Solutions

Disease

Early Blight

A fungal disease causing brown spots with concentric rings on leaves, affecting plant health and yield.

üí° Solution:

Remove infected leaves, apply copper-based fungicide, ensure proper spacing for air circulation, and use resistant varieties.

Disease

Powdery Mildew

Appears as white, powdery coating on leaves, stems, and flowers, reducing photosynthesis.

üí° Solution:

Apply neem oil or sulfur spray, improve air circulation, avoid overhead watering, and remove affected plant parts.

Disease

Leaf Spot

Spots on leaves caused by bacteria or fungi, leading to premature leaf drop and reduced growth.

üí° Solution:

Remove affected leaves, use fungicides, avoid overhead watering, and rotate crops.

Disease

Root Rot

Caused by overwatering or poorly drained soil, roots decay and plants wilt or die.

üí° Solution:

Improve soil drainage, reduce watering, and use fungicides like mefenoxam or phosphonates.

Disease

Downy Mildew

Causes yellow or white patches on upper leaf surfaces, leading to stunted growth.

üí° Solution:

Use resistant varieties, apply fungicides, and ensure proper air flow around plants.

Disease

Rust

Fungal infection showing orange, red, or brown pustules on leaves and stems.

üí° Solution:

Remove and destroy infected parts, use sulfur or copper-based fungicides, rotate crops.

Disease

Anthracnose

Dark, sunken lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits caused by fungi.

üí° Solution:

Use disease-free seeds, apply fungicides, and avoid wet foliage.

Disease

Mosaic Virus

Causes mottled green and yellow patterns on leaves, leading to stunted growth.

üí° Solution:

Remove infected plants, control insect vectors like aphids, and use virus-resistant seeds.

Disease

Bacterial Wilt

Bacteria block water flow in plant stems causing sudden wilting and death.

üí° Solution:

Remove affected plants, sterilize tools, rotate crops, and manage insects like beetles.

Disease

Fusarium Wilt

Fungal disease causing yellowing and wilting of leaves, mostly affecting vascular tissue.

üí° Solution:

Use resistant varieties, solarize soil, rotate crops, and improve drainage.

Disease

Damping Off

Fungal disease affecting seedlings, causing sudden wilting and stem rot near soil line.

üí° Solution:

Use sterile soil, avoid overwatering, and treat with fungicides during early stages.

Disease

Black Spot

Common rose disease causing black spots on leaves and leaf drop.

üí° Solution:

Prune infected areas, improve air circulation, and apply fungicides like chlorothalonil.
